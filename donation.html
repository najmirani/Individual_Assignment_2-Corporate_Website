Donation Instructions
For Donors

Your Support Directly Feeds and Shelters a Child

Persatuan Kebajikan Anak Yatim Nur Hidayah has no government grant and no permanent corporate sponsor. Every single month, the home relies entirely on the generosity of individuals, communities and organisations to cover its operating costs of approximately RM 15,000 — covering meals, utilities, school fees, staff salaries, medical care and more.

Donors should always confirm current needs directly with the organisation before making contributions, especially for in-kind donations of goods.

⚠️ Do not transfer money to any bank account unless the account name and number are verified through an official source. Always confirm with the chairperson, Mohd Yussairie Mohd Saad, through the official Facebook page at facebook.com/RAYNurHidayahKpgCheh before donating.

Monthly Operating Cost

Nur Hidayah's monthly operating cost is approximately RM 15,000. This covers utilities, the salaries of six staff members, meals, pocket money for children, medical expenses and transport. The home receives no fixed government allocation.

Volunteer Support

Other Ways To Contribute

Support does not have to be financial. Volunteers are welcome to assist through tutoring, cooking sessions, sports coaching, arts and craft activities, motivational talks and general event support.

Group visits from schools, universities, corporations or NGOs are warmly welcomed. However, visitors should always contact the organisation first. Turning up without notice may disturb the home's daily schedule and the children's routines.

Organisations or groups that wish to donate cooked food, conduct fundraising events or carry out gotong-royong activities at the home are also encouraged to reach out in advance to coordinate properly.

Responsible Giving

Donor Checklist

Before contributing, please keep these important reminders in mind to ensure your donation reaches those in need safely and responsibly.

✓ Confirm the home currently accepts the items you wish to donate.
✓ Do not donate expired food, damaged goods or used personal hygiene items.
✓ Respect the children's privacy at all times during any visit.
✓ Always ask permission before taking or sharing photos of the children.
✓ Verify bank account details only through the official Facebook page.
✓ Keep your receipt or transaction proof after any bank transfer.
