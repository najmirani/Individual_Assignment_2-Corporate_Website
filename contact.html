Directions

How to Get There

Nur Hidayah is situated in a village setting in Bukit Gantang, accessible by car from Taiping or Changkat Jering town.

🚗 By Car from Taiping

  1. Head north from Taiping town centre via Jalan Changkat Jering
  2. Pass through Changkat Jering town
  3. Continue towards Bukit Gantang
  4. Turn into Kampung Cheh — look for signage or contact the chairperson for precise directions

🚌 By Bus from Taiping

  1. Take a local bus from Taiping Bus Terminal heading towards Bukit Gantang
  2. Alight at Kampung Cheh / Bukit Gantang
  3. A short ride by motorcycle taxi (kapcai) or hired transport to the home
  4. Always confirm the exact stopping point with the chairperson before departure
📞 Important: Always Call Before Visiting

Please contact Chairperson Mohd Yussairie Mohd Saad before making your way to Nur Hidayah. Unannounced visits may disrupt the children's prayers, meals and study routines. A prior call ensures a warm and prepared welcome.
