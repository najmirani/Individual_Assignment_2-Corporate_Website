A Home Built on Compassion for Orphaned Children in Perak

Since 2004, Persatuan Kebajikan Anak Yatim Nur Hidayah has been a safe, loving shelter for orphaned and underprivileged children in Kampung Cheh, Bukit Gantang, Changkat Jering, Perak — sustained entirely by the generosity of the public.

Who We Are

A Home That Grew from One Man's Act of Kindness

Persatuan Kebajikan Anak Yatim Nur Hidayah began as a single act of compassion by Allahyarham Abd Manaf Tahir, who in 2004 opened his home in Kampung Cheh, Bukit Gantang, to orphaned and abandoned children in the surrounding community.

Driven by his family and encouraged by the villagers, what started as a personal responsibility grew into a formal welfare organisation. Today, under the leadership of Chairperson Mohd Yussairie Mohd Saad, Nur Hidayah continues to care for more than 30 children — providing shelter, meals, education, and above all, a family.

Be the Reason a Child Smiles Today

Nur Hidayah survives entirely on public donations. There is no government funding and no permanent corporate sponsor. Your contribution — however large or small — directly feeds, educates and shelters a child in need.
